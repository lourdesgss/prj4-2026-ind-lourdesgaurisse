This week was mostly dedicated to practical experimentation and analysis. After the insights gained during the EDA period, I started a separate investigation into the calculation of noise, defined as 

Noise = Var(SD | same (Colour, Chip, Nozzle, V, F_r, dt2, Coverage))

which I developed in a [separate notebook](../evidence/notebooks/4-EDA-noise.pdf).

During the process, the calculation of a noise threshold revealed some issues related to data consistency, as many parameter combinations did not contain the expected six repeated sheet measurements.

This raised attention to a further need for more solid data cleaning, for which I needed a deeper understanding of both the dataset structure and the relationship with the physical printing system.

## Reflection

During this week, I regained direction within the project, and while the original objective got sidetracked, it raised a more important issue to be addressed. 

It allowed me to see some gaps in my current understanding of the data and to look for ways to address those issues in order to avoid misinterpretations of data or incorrect aggregations across different dimensions.
